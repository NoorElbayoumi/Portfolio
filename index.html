I am a 21-year-old college student at the University of North Carolina at Greensboro majoring in Information Systems & Supply Chain Management, my name is Nooreldin Elbayoumi, but you can call me Noor for short! I am in that exciting and somewhat nerve-wracking phase of life where the future is wide open, and anything is possible.

From a young age, I was drawn to creative fields like animation, graphic design, web design, and game design. However, I soon discovered their intense oversaturation, leading me to explore alternative paths that could combine my passion for creativity.

When I was introduced to statistics in high school, I thought it was rather interesting. I could gather data and make an interpretation of what the future could hold. From there, I did extensive research to find the perfect balance between using statistics and letting my creativity show.

That's when I fell in love with data analysis. To me, it's the perfect balance between the logical, analytical side of my brain and the creative, artistic side. I enjoy collecting, cleaning, and validating data and presenting it in simple, user-friendly dashboards that help people or businesses make better, organized, data-driven decisions.

As I near the end of my university studies, I am committed to continuing to develop my skills and experience in this field. I am excited to take on freelance projects, update my website, and connect with others who share my passion for data analysis.

If you're looking for an upcoming data analyst who can help you turn your data into insights and actionable information, please don't hesitate to reach out. I would love to chat with you and see how we can work together to achieve your goals!
